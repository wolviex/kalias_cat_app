This storyboard focuses on **Noodles the Cat**, who embodies high energy and physical play. To keep the spelling lesson from feeling like a "lecture," we integrate it into a game of **Laser Tag**. 

Noodles will chase a laser pointer that "paints" letters onto the floor, requiring Kalia to guide him to spell specific words to earn a toy.

---

### Producer’s Note on DDA (Dynamic Difficulty Adjustment)

* **Explorer (3-4):** * **Task:** Letter Matching. The game asks for only *one* letter at a time (e.g., "Find the letter **N** for Noodles"). 
    * **Mechanic:** There are no distractor letters. Any letter Noodles touches is the right one to build confidence.
* **Navigator (5-6):** * **Task:** 3–4 letter CVC (Consonant-Vowel-Consonant) words like **CAT**, **HAT**, **BED**.
    * **Mechanic:** One or two distractor letters are present. The order of pouncing matters.
* **Master (7-8):** * **Task:** Longer words (5-7 letters) or "Sight Words."
    * **Mechanic:** The letters move! They slowly drift across the floor like bubbles, requiring the player to time the laser lead-in so Noodles intercepts the moving target.
