### The Level-Up Concept: "The Magical Trunk"
To wrap the concept together, we need a visual for the rewards.

**Scene:** Kalia’s bedroom. A large, glowing trunk sits in the corner.
**Mechanic:** When the "Purr-gress" bar fills, the trunk bounces.
**Interaction:** The player taps the trunk. It bursts open with "Kalia Sparks."
**Reward:** Three cards appear. The player chooses one:
1.  **Noodles' Cape:** A superhero cape for Noodles.
2.  **Robot’s Glow-Antenna:** A neon green skin for Robot Cat.
3.  **Kalia’s Cat-Ear Headband:** A matching accessory for the player.


This storyboard focuses on the **"Reward Loop."** To keep children engaged, the transition from "doing work" (learning) to "getting prizes" (customization) needs to feel magical and high-signal.

### The "Purr-gress" Leveling System
* **XP Collection:** Every successful minigame interaction emits "Heart Sparks" (Care XP) or "Star Sparks" (Learning XP).
* **The Level Bar:** Located at the top of the screen, styled like a colorful yarn ball that unrolls as it fills.
* **The Milestone:** Every time the yarn ball completely unrolls, the game transitions to **"The Magical Trunk"** sequence.

### Expanded Leveling Mechanics

To maintain long-term interest, the leveling system uses **Tiered Milestones**:

#### **1. The "Bonding" Levels (1-10)**
* **Focus:** Core items. 
* **Unlocks:** Basic colors for Kalia’s clothes and simple toys (yarn ball, basic bowl, cardboard box).
* **Educational Goal:** Building the habit of daily check-ins.

#### **2. The "Expert Care" Levels (11-25)**
* **Focus:** Thematic sets.
* **Unlocks:** "Matching Sets." If the player collects all pieces of a set (e.g., The Space Set), they unlock a special **Super-Animation**.
    * *Example:* Equipping Kalia, Noodles, and Robot Cat in "Space Suits" unlocks the "Zero-G Play" background where they float.
* **Educational Goal:** Pattern recognition and completing sets.

#### **3. The "Discovery" Levels (26+ )**
* **Focus:** World Expansion.
* **Unlocks:** New "Rooms" in the house or an "Outdoor Garden."
* **Educational Goal:** Broadening the scope of the math and spelling games (e.g., spelling names of flowers in the garden).

### Summary of Rewards by Category

| Category | Item Examples | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Kalia’s Gear** | Hoodies, Tutus, Capes, Boots. | Visual self-expression for the player. |
| **Cat Costumes** | Bowties, Glasses, Wings, Hats. | Hilarious visual rewards that make the cats feel distinct. |
| **Interactive Toys** | Laser 2.0, Automatic Feeder, Music Box. | These actually change the minigames (e.g., a Music Box makes the "Calming" game easier). |
| **Status Boosters** | "Golden Kibble," "Super Soft Brush." | Instantly refills a cat’s emotion/hunger meter if the player is in a hurry. |

**The "Winning Solution" for the parent:**
The leveling system is strictly performance-based (learning games) or care-based (EQ). There are **no "pay-to-win" shortcuts**, ensuring the child understands that effort and care lead to growth.
